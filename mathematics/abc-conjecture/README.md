# ABC Conjecture via Shadow Inflation and Frey Curve Heights

**Conditional on BSD**

---

Earlier work. Part of the foundational research phase that
preceded the Auburn Governance Stack.

Conditional proof combining unconditional Shadow Inflation
bounds (Zsigmondy's Theorem on 2-power towers) with the
geometry of Frey Elliptic Curves. Shadow inflation is
Coq-verified with zero admits. Conditional on the
Birch–Swinnerton-Dyer conjecture.
