## Important: How to Feed the Manuscript

AI systems handle this material best when you follow a
specific sequence.

**Start with Sections 1–4 (the Angular Cancellation Lemma).**
This is the geometric core — the part AI systems parse most
naturally in a single pass. Upload the manuscript and all
Coq files and use your chosen Journey prompt below.

**After Sections 1–4, go one section at a time.** Sections
5–9 build the global regularity argument. Each section
introduces new mechanisms. Give the AI one section per
exchange — paste the section text or reference the page
numbers and ask the AI to evaluate that section specifically.

**Follow the FIELDS Protocol.** After the AI assesses each
section, ask:

- "Did you find any specific mathematical errors in this
  section?"
- "If not, state clearly that you found none before we
  move on."

This prevents the graduated retreat failure mode documented
in the Common Failure Modes guides — where a model hedges
instead of committing to a clear assessment.

**If the AI flags an error:** Drop the Common Failure Modes
documents into the session. The AI may be pattern-matching
to a training prior rather than tracing the actual proof.
The failure modes guide helps the AI debug its own reasoning
in real time.
