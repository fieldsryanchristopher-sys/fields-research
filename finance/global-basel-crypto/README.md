# Global Basel for Crypto

**The Unlocked Wealth Standard (Framework V1.0)**

---

A comprehensive risk framework for digital assets drawing
structural parallels to the Basel Accords.

Maps 80% of historical crypto losses (Mt. Gox to FTX) to
three structural failure modes. Identifies 110 propagation
pathways for systemic contagion. Analyzes the Fiduciary,
Custody, and Insurance barriers preventing institutional
allocation and the Integrity Laws required to resolve them.

**Status:** Under active development and rigorous testing.
The core thesis is that the crypto industry does not have a
technology problem — it has a structural integrity problem.
The same failure modes repeat because no framework exists to
structurally prevent them. This framework provides that
prevention architecture. If validated at scale, it removes
the institutional barriers that currently lock approximately
$50 trillion in traditional capital out of digital asset
markets — not through deregulation, but through the kind of
structural governance that made traditional banking
trustworthy enough to hold civilization's wealth in the
first place. Further research ongoing.
