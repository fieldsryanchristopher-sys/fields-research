# The Auburn Framework

**Geometric Constraints on Critical Transitions
in Physical Systems**

---

A geometric risk engine derived from the 3D incompressible
Navier–Stokes equations that predicts rupture in seismic and
atmospheric systems with 86% blind-test accuracy.

This preprint contains the theoretical basis and validation
data. Operational specifications, parameter calibrations, and
production-ready pipelines are redacted and available only for
institutional partnership or licensing.
