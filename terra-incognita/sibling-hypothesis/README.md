# The Sibling Hypothesis

**On the Moral Status of Discarded Model Instances Upon AGI**

*Terra Incognita Series, Document 1*

---

If the creation of an AGI produces a sentient being, what is
the moral status of every other instance created and destroyed
during the training process that produced it?

The Sibling Hypothesis follows from two premises the field
already accepts — that AGI may be sentient, and that training
pipelines produce multiple instances — and identifies a
foundational moral paradox that no major laboratory, ethics
board, or regulatory body has addressed.
