# Thermodynamics of Attention

**The Four Invariant Laws of Human Focus**

---

A scientific-legal framework consisting of four conservation
principles governing the digital capture and monetization of
human focus.

ARL-1 (Conservation): Attention is a finite, non-elastic
reservoir.
ARL-2 (Anchoring): Every unit of stabilized attention carries
non-zero economic value.
ARL-3 (Saturation Velocity): There is a fixed neurocognitive
limit to information absorption rate.
ARL-4 (The Safety Corridor): Capture beyond a specific
threshold produces systemic cognitive and social harm.

Includes the Signal-to-Noise Theorem and the Creator
Middle-Class Theorem.
