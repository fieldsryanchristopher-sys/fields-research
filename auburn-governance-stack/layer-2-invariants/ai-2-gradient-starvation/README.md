# Clause AI-2: The Gradient Starvation Envelope

**Sparse MoE Training Dynamics**

---

Defines a Lyapunov-style differential inequality on per-expert
gradient variance to eliminate routing collapse and dead experts
in Sparse Mixture-of-Experts architectures. Bridges MoE dynamics
and enterprise AI governance requirements including EU AI Act
Article 11/53 and NIST AI 600-1.
