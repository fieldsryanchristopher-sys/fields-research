# Autonomous AI Agents in Regulated Financial Services

**Governance Framework for Agentic Systems Under DORA
and the EU AI Act**

---

The first published governance framework for autonomous AI
agents in regulated financial services. Three tiers of agent
autonomy. Article-by-article DORA analysis identifying thirteen
provisions where the regulation fails for agentic systems.
Resolves the structural paradox between AI Act Article 14
human oversight and autonomous operation.

Six-category Agentic Risk Taxonomy (ART-1 through ART-6).
Three uniquely agentic with no analogue in compositional risk.
Four architecture-specific compliance profiles for trade
execution, AML investigation, claims processing, and customer
onboarding.
