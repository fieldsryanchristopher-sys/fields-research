# CRSA-1 EU Edition

**Compositional Safety Compliance Profile for
EU AI Act Regulation 2024/1689**

---

The first published compositional safety compliance framework
for multi-model AI systems under the EU AI Act. Maps 38
distinct AI Act obligations to compositional implications.
Identifies five critical regulatory gaps. Eight compositional
risk categories. Five architecture-specific compliance profiles
for RAG pipelines, agentic systems, ensembles, cascade/routing,
and MCP-based multi-agent systems.

First published model contract terms for Article 25(4)
multi-vendor composed systems.
