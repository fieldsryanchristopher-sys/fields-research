# Auburn Governance Stack (AGS-1)

**A Layered Architecture Specification for Verifiable AI
Compliance**

---

The first published layered architecture for verifiable AI
compliance. Hourglass model composing hardware integrity,
model health, and supply chain provenance through the MAI-1
composition waist. Maps to EU AI Act, DORA, NIST AI RMF,
FDA SaMD, CMMC, and insurance underwriting requirements.

Forty-five documents across seven layers. Seventeen published.
Complete critical path delivered.
