# Lyapunov Stability Envelopes for Speculative Decoding

**Formalizing Rejection-Rate Compliance**

---

Applies Lyapunov stability analysis to speculative decoding
instability in LLMs. Treats token rejection rate as a dynamical
state variable. Guarantees bounded recovery time with explicit
exponential convergence, side-channel mitigation defeating >90%
accuracy timing fingerprinting, and regulatory robustness for
EU AI Act Article 15 and Basel SR 11-7 financial determinism.
