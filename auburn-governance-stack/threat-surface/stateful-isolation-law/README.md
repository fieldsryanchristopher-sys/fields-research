# The Stateful Isolation Law

**Formal Contamination Bounds for Multi-Principal AI Systems**

---

Catalogs 25+ demonstrated attacks including PROMPTPEEK (99%
prompt reconstruction), LeftoverLocals (181 MB/query leakage),
and Agentic Confused Deputy (82.4% inter-agent compromise).
Defines the Stateful Isolation Law — a unified formal framework
comprising a contamination functional parameterized by Rényi
divergence, six enforceable clauses, and compliance tiers
mapping to GDPR, EU AI Act, and FedRAMP.
