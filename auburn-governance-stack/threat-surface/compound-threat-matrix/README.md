# The Compound Threat Matrix

**Structural Vulnerabilities in AI Systems Requiring
Unified Governance Infrastructure**

---

Documents over 150 verified incidents, regulatory actions,
and quantified impacts across six functional domains of AI
deployment (2024–February 2026). Identifies compound
interaction pathways through which vulnerabilities in one
domain propagate into and amplify failures in others. Five
primary cascade pathways traced with confirmed evidence at
every step. Derives five minimum structural requirements
for any adequate governance response.
