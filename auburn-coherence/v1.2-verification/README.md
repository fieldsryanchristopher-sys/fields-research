# Auburn Coherence Framework v1.2

**February 2026 Prediction Verification:
Seven-Region Global Assessment**

---

Verifies predictions issued February 5, 2026 against observed
events through February 28, 2026 across seven global regions.

Of 28 discrete predictions: 20 confirmed or confirmed early
(71.4%), five exhibited partial verification with correct
mechanism identification, three remain within open windows.
No predictions contradicted by observed outcomes.

Associated events: estimated USD $12–15 billion in combined
economic losses, more than 200 fatalities.
